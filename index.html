About the Artist

Ink is my
language

I'm Oliwier Rynkiewicz — a London-based tattoo artist with over a decade of experience turning ideas into permanent art. Every piece I create is designed from scratch, built around your vision, your body, and your story.

From bold traditional work to intricate fine-line pieces, each session is approached with obsessive attention to detail. No templates. No flash. Just art that stays with you for life.

Specialties

What I Do

01
Black & Grey
Timeless monochromatic work with smooth gradients and precise shading — the gold standard of tattoo artistry.
02
Fine Line
Delicate single-needle precision. Elegant minimal designs that carry maximum impact in a minimal footprint.
03
Traditional
Bold outlines, saturated colour, iconic imagery — rooted in the rich history of classic tattoo tradition.
04
Watercolour
Painterly, fluid, vivid. Abstract splashes and soft washes that push the boundaries of skin as canvas.
05
Realism
Photorealistic portraits and nature rendered with stunning depth, dimension, and anatomical accuracy.
06
Geometric
Sacred geometry, mandala work, and precise linework that brings mathematical beauty to the human form.
